# IoT Market Research Skill

Conduct thorough market research for IoT products including smart devices, connected home products, industrial IoT, wearables, and embedded systems.

## When to Use This Skill

- Analyzing IoT market size and growth projections
- Identifying competitor landscape in IoT space
- Understanding customer segments for connected devices
- Researching regulatory requirements (FCC, CE, privacy)
- Evaluating technology trends and adoption curves
- Assessing partnership and ecosystem opportunities

## Research Framework

### 1. Market Size Analysis

When analyzing IoT market size:

```
1. Define the specific IoT segment:
   - Smart Home (thermostats, security, lighting)
   - Industrial IoT (sensors, predictive maintenance)
   - Consumer Wearables (fitness, health monitoring)
   - Connected Vehicles
   - Smart Cities/Infrastructure

2. Gather data points:
   - Total Addressable Market (TAM)
   - Serviceable Addressable Market (SAM)
   - Serviceable Obtainable Market (SOM)
   - CAGR projections (typically 5-year outlook)

3. Key metrics to find:
   - Unit shipments by category
   - Average selling price (ASP) trends
   - Revenue by region (NA, EU, APAC)
   - Market share by key players
```

### 2. Competitive Analysis Framework

For IoT competitor research:

```
| Analysis Area | What to Research |
|--------------|------------------|
| Direct Competitors | Products with same function/price point |
| Indirect Competitors | Alternative solutions to same problem |
| Platform Players | Amazon Alexa, Google Home, Apple HomeKit |
| Component Suppliers | Chipsets, modules, sensors |
| Ecosystem Partners | App developers, integrators |

Key competitive dimensions:
- Feature comparison matrix
- Pricing tiers and strategies
- Distribution channels
- Patent landscape
- Funding and M&A activity
```

### 3. Customer Segmentation

IoT customer segments to analyze:

**B2C IoT Products:**
- Early Adopters (tech enthusiasts)
- Smart Home Enthusiasts (convenience-driven)
- Security-Focused Consumers
- Energy-Conscious Households
- Accessibility/Health Needs

**B2B IoT Products:**
- Enterprise IT Buyers
- Operations/Facilities Managers
- Industry-Specific Verticals
- System Integrators
- OEM Partners

### 4. Technology Trend Analysis

Key technology areas to research:

```
Connectivity:
- WiFi 6/6E adoption
- Matter protocol adoption
- Bluetooth LE / Thread
- 5G IoT applications
- LoRaWAN/Sigfox for LPWAN

Processing:
- Edge AI capabilities
- MCU vs MPU trends
- Power consumption benchmarks

Security:
- Hardware security modules
- OTA update capabilities
- Privacy regulations (GDPR, CCPA)

Interoperability:
- Matter certification
- Works with Alexa/Google/Apple
- IFTTT integration
```

### 5. Regulatory Landscape

Compliance areas to research:

```
Safety & EMC:
- FCC (US wireless)
- CE marking (EU)
- UL/CSA safety
- RoHS/REACH (materials)

Wireless:
- Bluetooth SIG certification
- WiFi Alliance certification
- Z-Wave Alliance

Privacy:
- GDPR implications
- CCPA requirements
- Data localization rules

Industry-Specific:
- FDA (health devices)
- UL 2900 (cybersecurity)
- ISO 27001 (enterprise)
```

- LinkedIn for company/people insights

## Best Practices

1. **Triangulate data** - Use multiple sources to validate market figures
2. **Date your research** - IoT moves fast; note when data was gathered
3. **Consider ecosystems** - IoT products rarely stand alone
4. **Map the value chain** - Understand component to end-user flow
5. **Track standards** - Protocol adoption affects market dynamics
6. **Monitor crowdfunding** - Kickstarter/Indiegogo for emerging competitors
7. **Review patent filings** - Early indicator of competitor direction
