## Company Overview

**Tagline:** "One Seat. Every Journey."

**Mission:** To simplify parenting by creating the world's most intelligent modular baby seating system that grows with your child and gives parents peace of mind.

---

## Product: The **NestPod Pro**

### What is NestPod Pro?

A revolutionary modular smart baby seating system that seamlessly transforms between:
- **Car Seat** (rear-facing infant, forward-facing toddler)
- **High Chair** (dining table integration)
- **Stroller Seat** (connects to wheeled base)
- **Stationary Rocker** (home use)
- **Travel Carrier** (portable)

### Key Differentiator

Unlike Doona (car seat + stroller only) or traditional systems requiring multiple separate products, NestPod Pro is **ONE core seat module** with interchangeable bases and accessories, integrated with **smart sensors and AI**.

---

## Smart Technology Features

### 1. Safety Monitoring Suite

| Feature | Sensor | Function |
|---------|--------|----------|
| Breathing Detection | Piezoelectric mat | Alerts if breathing pauses >20 seconds |
| Temperature Monitor | Thermistor array | Warns of overheating; triggers cooling |
| Position Tracking | Accelerometer + Gyro | Detects unsafe positioning/rollover |
| Harness Check | Load cells | Confirms proper harness tension |
| Car Seat Installation | Tilt sensor + Load | Verifies correct angle and secure attachment |

### 2. Comfort Intelligence

| Feature | Technology | Benefit |
|---------|------------|---------|
| SootheSync | Speakers + vibration | Plays white noise, heartbeat sounds on demand |
| Climate Control | Temp sensors + fans | Maintains optimal temperature |
| Ride Comfort | Accelerometer | Detects rough terrain; adjusts suspension |
| Sleep Tracking | Motion sensors | Reports sleep patterns to app |

### 3. Connected Parenting App

**NestTech App Features:**
- Real-time vital sign monitoring
- Historical sleep and activity data
- Temperature and humidity alerts
- Car seat installation verification
- Stroller location tracking (GPS)
- Multi-caregiver access
- Pediatrician data sharing (opt-in)

### 4. AI-Powered Features

- **CryDecode**: Analyzes cry patterns to suggest if baby is hungry, tired, or uncomfortable
- **Sleep Optimization**: Learns baby's patterns, suggests optimal nap times
- **Safety Predictor**: Alerts to potential issues before they become problems
