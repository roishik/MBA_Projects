# Option 3: MobilityPro - Smart Elderly Mobility Chair

## Company Overview

### Company Name: **MobilityPro Health**

**Tagline:** "Independence. Dignity. Peace of Mind."

**Mission:** To restore independence and dignity to seniors and mobility-impaired individuals through intelligent wheelchair technology that enables autonomous navigation while providing continuous health monitoring for caregivers.

---

## Product: **AutoGlide Smart Wheelchair**

### What is AutoGlide?

A next-generation smart wheelchair that combines:
- **Autonomous navigation** - point-to-point travel without caregiver assistance
- **Integrated health monitoring** - vital signs tracked and reported in real-time
- **Fall and anomaly detection** - immediate alerts to caregivers
- **Connectivity ecosystem** - integrates with smart home, facility, and healthcare systems

### Target Users

| Segment | Description | Key Needs |
|---------|-------------|-----------|
| Independent Seniors | Aging at home with limited mobility | Autonomy, safety, dignity |
| Nursing Home Residents | Long-term care facility users | Staff efficiency, safety monitoring |
| Rehabilitation Patients | Temporary mobility needs | Progress tracking, independence |
| Disability Community | Permanent mobility assistance | Customization, reliability |

## Smart Technology Features

### 1. Autonomous Navigation

| Feature | Technology | Function |
|---------|------------|----------|
| Point-to-Point Travel | LiDAR + Computer Vision | Navigate independently in mapped environments |
| Obstacle Avoidance | Ultrasonic + IR sensors | Detect and avoid obstacles in real-time |
| Indoor Positioning | UWB beacons | Sub-meter accuracy indoors |
| Elevator Integration | API connections | Automatically call and enter elevators |
| Path Learning | ML algorithms | Learn preferred routes over time |

### 2. Health Monitoring Suite

| Vital Sign | Sensor | Alert Threshold |
|------------|--------|-----------------|
| Heart Rate | PPG in armrest | <40 or >120 BPM |
| Blood Oxygen | Pulse oximetry | <90% SpO2 |
| Skin Temperature | Thermistor | >38.5Â°C (fever) |
| Respiratory Rate | Chest motion | <8 or >30 breaths/min |
| Blood Pressure | Oscillometric cuff (optional) | User-defined |

### 3. Safety & Fall Detection

| Feature | Technology | Response |
|---------|------------|----------|
| Fall Detection | Accelerometer + Gyroscope | Immediate caregiver alert |
| Inactivity Alert | Motion sensors | Alert after configurable period |
| Geofencing | GPS + Indoor positioning | Alert if leaving safe zone |
| SOS Button | Manual emergency | Direct caregiver/911 connection |

### 4. Connected Care Platform

**MobilityPro Care Portal:**
- Real-time location dashboard
- Vital sign trend visualization
- Alert management and escalation
- Care team coordination tools
- Family member access (permission-based)
- EHR integration (Epic, Cerner)

## Regulatory Strategy

### FDA Classification

**Target:** Class II Medical Device (510(k) pathway)

**Predicate Devices:**
- Powered wheelchairs (existing 510(k)s)
- Remote patient monitoring devices

**Timeline:** 12-18 months for clearance

### Medicare Reimbursement

**Strategy:** Position as Power Mobility Device + RPM

**Current Codes:**
- K0856: Group 3 Power Wheelchair
- RPM codes: 99453, 99454, 99457

**Estimated Reimbursement:** $3,000-5,000 (wheelchair) + $60-150/month (RPM)
