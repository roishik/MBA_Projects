## 7. Regulatory Landscape

### Baby Products

- **US**: CPSC (Consumer Product Safety Commission) standards
- **EU**: EN 1888 (strollers), ECE R44/R129 (car seats)
- **FDA**: Does not approve consumer "SIDS monitors" - important positioning consideration

### Healthcare/Mobility

- **FDA**: Medical device clearance required for health claims
- **ADA**: Accessibility compliance for public use
- **ISO 7176**: Wheelchair safety standards

### Venue Technology

- **GDPR/CCPA**: Data privacy for audience analytics
- **ADA**: Accessible seating requirements
