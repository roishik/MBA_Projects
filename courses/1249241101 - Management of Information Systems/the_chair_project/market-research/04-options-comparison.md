## Final Recommendation: **Option 1 - NestTech**

**Rationale:**

1. **Fastest Path to Market** - Consumer products don't require enterprise sales cycles or FDA approval
2. **Proven Demand** - Doona and 4moms demonstrate market appetite for smart baby products
3. **Premium Pricing Power** - Parents pay for safety and convenience
4. **Emotional Brand Opportunity** - Baby products create lasting customer relationships
5. **Manageable Technical Risk** - Sensors and app technology are well-understood
6. **Lower Capital Requirements** - $3M vs $4.5-5M for other options
7. **Manufacturing Leverage** - Best utilizes existing Comfy Chairs capabilities

However, all three options are viable and defensible choices for the presentation.
